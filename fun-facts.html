Poodles are super smart, in fact, they are the second smartest dog in the world.

Poodles are hypoallergenic: they don't shed. That's why they are often bred with other dogs, to to make breeds such as a Yorkie Poo, Labradoodle, and Goldendoodle as well as several others breeds.

Poodles are France's national dog - but they're actually from Germany, where they were called Pudel, which is German for "puddle."

Poodles were bred to hunt ducks in the water. Their famously fancy cut was actually made so that they could swim efficiently in the cold water (or "puddle")!
